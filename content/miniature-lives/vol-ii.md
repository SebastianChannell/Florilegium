# Lives and Devotions

## July 1 — B. Giovanni Colombini.

**Virtue:** Love of God.

### Life

Giovanni Colombini was a noble citizen of Siena who turned from worldly prosperity to the ardent love of God.

### Love of God.

The love of God makes all burdens light and gives sweetness to every act of service.

"My God and my all."
