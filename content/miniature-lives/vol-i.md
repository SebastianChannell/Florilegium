# Lives and Devotions

## January 1 — S. Fulgentius, Bishop.

**Virtue:** Patience through Faith.

### Life

S. Fulgentius was born in Africa of a noble family. From childhood he loved sacred learning and the service of God.

He became bishop amid persecution, and bore exile and hardship with a patient heart fixed upon Christ.

### Patience through Faith.

Each year may bring us fresh changes; faith teaches us to receive them from the hand of God.

"Lord, give me patience, and increase my faith."

An Arian priest betrayed Fulgentius, yet the saint answered injury with meekness and prayer.

"For patience is necessary for you, that, doing the will of God, you may receive the promise." Heb. x. 36.

## June 30 — The First Holy Martyrs of the Roman Church.

**Virtue:** Constancy.

### Life

The first martyrs of Rome suffered under Nero after the burning of the city.

### Constancy.

Let the remembrance of the martyrs strengthen us in lesser trials.
